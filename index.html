About the Author

Maren Blackthorn

Maren Blackthorn writes dark academia romantasy and thrillers—stories set in candlelit libraries and crumbling halls, where ambition, desire, and danger are never far apart. Her novels blend slow-burn romance with magic, secrets, and the kind of tension that keeps the pages turning past midnight.
